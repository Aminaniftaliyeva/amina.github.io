Project Overview

The Hour of AI makes teaching AI literacy easy, engaging, and fun. This initiative empowers students to become the next generation of innovators with AI.

Event Details

As part of Team 43, we hosted this event at Lachin School No.45 in Bilajari. We taught children from 6th grade algorithms and how to apply AI, introducing them to fundamental concepts of artificial intelligence and computational thinking.

Key Features

  • Teaching AI literacy to 6th grade students
  • Introduction to algorithms and AI applications
  • Engaging and interactive learning experience
  • Empowering students to become future innovators
  • Community outreach at Lachin School No.45 in Bilajari

Technologies & Tools

This educational event utilized various AI learning platforms and educational tools to create an engaging and accessible learning experience for young students.

Teaching Materials

We used interactive Microsoft Bug arena where little Bug have to fill rectangular area using certain algorithm.

Type of Algorithms

  • Random Algorithm: Create an AI algorithm that will make your bug walk in random patterns
  • Square Algorithm: Code your AI bug to draw squares around the screen
  • Back and Forth Algorithm: Code your bug's AI algorithm to move back and forth across the screen

Try yourself

Impact

Through this Hour of AI event, we successfully introduced AI concepts to 6th grade students, helping them understand how algorithms work and how AI can be applied in real-world scenarios. The event aimed to inspire the next generation of innovators and make AI education accessible and enjoyable for young learners.
